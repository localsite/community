Steps for creating your entry

1. Identify your community of interest

A community can be a neighborhood, city, county, region of a state, or multiple states and may have a theme associated with it.

2. Explore our community apps and learn how to make your own

Review the apps for your community of interest
. Read about how to create your own

3. Join or form a new team

Check if the community of interest already has formed a team. Join that team or start you own.
An ideal tema might involve community reps and include designers, HTML editors, coders and data contributors.

4. Create beautiful sites and add new functionality

Create new visuals for your community. Embed new or existing tools, components in your site
