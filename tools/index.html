US Environmentally Extended Input-Output (USEEIO) Model

BACKGROUND


  • The national model for estimating life cycle environmental performance of all US goods and services.
  • The model can help identify prosperous areas for development with fewer adverse impacts.
  • However, the model can be also be challenging for a community to leverage. That's where you come in!
